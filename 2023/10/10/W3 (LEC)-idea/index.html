Idea Generation (创意生成)

Thinking Hats (思考帽)

Idea (创意)

  • Imagery / Vision (图像/视觉)：描述创意的形象或愿景
  • An original thought (一个原始的想法)：描述一个完全独特的想法

Generation (生成)

  • Create (创建)：开始一个新的想法
  • Develop (发展)：进一步拓展或完善这个想法
  • Evolve (进化)：使想法不断变得更好
  • Cultivate (培养)：持续地支持和发展这个想法
  • Spontaneous (自发的)：描述快速且未经计划的想法产生过程
  • Concept / Premise (概念/前提)：描述创意的基本理念或出发点

Techniques (技巧)

  • Brain Storming (头脑风暴)
  • Random Stimulus (随机刺激)
  • Free Association (自由联想)
  • Alignment (对齐)
  • Refinement (精炼)
  • Thinking hats (思考帽)
  • SCAMPER
  • Provocation (挑衅)

Brainstorming (头脑风暴)

  • Identify an area, e.g., Primary School App (确定一个领域，例如：小学应用)
  • Brain dump / iterate (头脑清空/迭代)
  • Spontaneous (自发的)
  • Non critical / non judgemental (非批判性/非评判性)
  • No filter (无过滤)
  • Review (回顾)
  • Refine (精炼)
  • Analyse (分析)
  • Thinking Hats (思考帽)
  • SWOT

Random Stimulus (随机刺激)

  • Multi-sensory (多感官)
    • Visual (视觉)
    • Auditory (听觉)
    • Tactile (触觉)
    • Gustatory (味觉)
    • Olfactory (嗅觉)
  • Creative Directors (创意总监)
  • Explore the diversity of the world (探索世界的多样性)

Sources for Random Stimulus (随机刺激的来源)

  • Dictionary (字典)
  • Newspaper (报纸)
  • Book (书籍)
  • Computer Program (计算机程序)
  • Random Picture (随机图片)
  • Advertisement (广告)
  • Flyer (传单)
  • Poster (海报)
  • Website (网站)

Thinking Hats (思考帽)

  • Intuition, hunches, gut reaction, feelings, emotions (直觉、预感、本能反应、情感、情绪)
  • Benefits, positives, value (好处、积极点、价值)
  • Creative, alternatives, possibilities (创意、替代方案、可能性)
  • Manage the thinking process, summary (管理思考过程、总结)
  • Caution, difficulties, weakness, risks (警惕、困难、弱点、风险)
  • Information, facts, data (信息、事实、数据)

Adapt Thinking Hats (适应思考帽)

  • User specific hats (特定用户的帽子)
    • Doctor (医生)
    • Engineer (工程师)
    • Scientist (科学家)
    • Designer (设计师)
    • Customer (客户)
    • Student (学生)
  • When making cars (制造汽车时)
    • Driver, passenger, adults, children, babies, mechanics, sales person, environmentalists, government (驾驶员、乘客、成人、儿童、婴儿、机械师、销售员、环保主义者、政府)

SCAMPER

  • Substitute (替代)
  • Combine (组合)
  • Adapt (适应)
  • Modify (修改)
  • Put to another use (改变用途)
  • Eliminate (消除)
  • Reverse (反转)

Provocation (挑衅)

  • Groundhog day (土拨鼠日)
  • Change the repetitive pattern of thinking (改变重复的思考模式)
  • Dream up an outrageous situation to break from the status quo (构想一个荒唐的情境以打破现状)，例如：A car with square wheels (一个有方形轮子的汽车)
  • Creates a new and fresh starting point to generate ideas from (为生成想法创造一个全新的起点)

Suggested Thinking Structure (建议的思考结构)

  • List general high level areas to work in (列出要工作的高级领域)
    • Retail (零售)
    • Food & Drink (食品与饮料)
    • Social Media (社交媒体)
  • Apply techniques to each area (将技巧应用到每一个领域)
    • Brainstorming (头脑风暴)
    • Thinking hats (思考帽)
    • Random word association and align to area (随机单词关联并对齐领域)
    • SCAMPER
    • Provocation (挑衅)

Tools (工具)

  • Mind mapping tool (思维导图工具)
  • Coggle - http://coggle.it
    • Used to document the process (用于记录过程)
  • Research other tools (研究其他工具)

Further Reading (进一步阅读)

  • Give your idea some legs: The positive effect of walking on creative thinking (走路对创意思维的积极效应)
  • Using your non-dominate hand to brush your teeth can promote creativity (使用非优势手刷牙可以促进创意)
  • The creative brain - how insight works (创意大脑 - 洞察如何工作)
    • Link here

Tutorial Class (教程课)

  • Start the creative thinking process (开始创意思考过程)
  • Explore the lateral thinking techniques (探索横向思考技巧)
    • Brainstorming (头脑风暴)
    • Random stimulus - word / imagery (随机刺激 - 单词/图像)
    • Thinking hats (思考帽)
    • SCAMPER
    • Provocation (挑衅)
  • Familiarise yourself with the tools (熟悉工具)
